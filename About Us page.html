Our Branches

Kandy Branch

Located in the heart of Kandy, our Fresh Foods Kandy branch supermarket offers a wide selection of fresh produce, pantry staples, and gourmet delicacies. With spacious aisles and friendly staff, shopping here is always a pleasant experience. Whether you're grabbing a quick snack or stocking up for a special occasion, we have everything you need to satisfy your cravings and culinary desires.

Colombo Branch

Located in the heart of Colombo, our Fresh Foods Colombo branch supermarket is a hub of activity and convenience. With ample parking and easy access from major roads, it's the perfect place to shop for groceries on the go. From everyday essentials to gourmet treats, our shelves are stocked with the finest quality products to cater to all tastes and preferences. Visit us today and experience shopping made simple and enjoyable.
